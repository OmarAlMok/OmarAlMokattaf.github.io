How to play the Game

Your guide to maneuver through the game and enjoy and win

How to play- Central City

  • Install VS Studio Code and install pygame
  • Open the game
  • Run the code to start the game.
  • On the main screen, press “Play” to begin.
  • The story will appear in a series of panels. Click anywhere on the screen to move to the next part of the story..
  • After the story panels, you will appear as the farmer standing before the enchanted maze.
  • Use the arrow keys — ↓, →, ↑, ←DOWN, RIGHT, UP, and LEFT — to move your character through the maze.
  • When you follow the correct path, you will encounter a riddle.
  • Click on the text input box to type your answer.
  • Press Enter to submit it.
  • Continue solving riddles and navigating the maze until you reach the princess.
  • Once you reach the princess, you’ll move to the final panel and win the game.
  • The game then loops back to the beginning, so you can play again if you’d like.
