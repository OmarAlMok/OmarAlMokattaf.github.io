About the Author

Hi! I’m Omar Elmekataf, a Mechatronics Engineering student with a passion for blending creativity, logic, and technology.

My background in engineering has fueled my curiosity for how things move, think, and interact—whether it’s a robot, a machine, or a line of code. Central City is a project born from that same curiosity. It combines storytelling, problem-solving, and interactive design to create an immersive experience where players must think critically, solve riddles, and navigate through a living maze.

The Online Library was also born out of that same curiosity and passion for software developing. How customer/server relationship worked and functioned has always been an interest of mine, and putting it into practice has expanded my horizons.

Through these projects, I explored the intersection of engineering and art—applying my technical skills in programming, systems thinking, and design to bring a world of imagination to life. My goal is to keep learning, building, and creating experiences that challenge both the mind and the heart.

Thank you for taking the time to explore my portfolio. I hope you enjoy experiencing the worlds I’ve created as much as I enjoyed building them!
