About the Author

Hi! I’m Omar Elmekataf, a Mechatronics Engineering student with a passion for blending creativity, logic, and technology.

My background in engineering has fueled my curiosity for how things move, think, and interact—whether it’s a robot, a machine, or a line of code. Central City is a project born from that same curiosity. It combines storytelling, problem-solving, and interactive design to create an immersive experience where players must think critically, solve riddles, and navigate through a living maze.

Through this project, I explored the intersection of engineering and art—applying my technical skills in programming, systems thinking, and design to bring a world of imagination to life. My goal is to keep learning, building, and creating experiences that challenge both the mind and the heart.
